About Me


I'm a Web Developer.

I'm Akshay Naresh Raikar. I hold a Master's degree in Data & Web Engineering from Chemnitz University of Technology, complemented by a Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Mumbai. As an enthusiastic and innovative Software Developer with 2-3 years of experience, I specialize in creating efficient web solutions and have a proven track record of designing and implementing robust applications. My technical skills span across both frontend and backend technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Node.js, Express.js, Python, Django, and databases such as Postgres, MySQL, Maria DB, and MongoDB. I have hands-on experience with cloud services like AWS, version control systems like Git, and container technologies including Docker, Kubernetes, ECS, and EKS. I am well-versed in CI/CD practices using Jenkins and have a solid foundation in Agile frameworks like Scrum and Kanban.
